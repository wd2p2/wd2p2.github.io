Submission instructions

Paper submission will be managed via the EDAS system. The paper should not exceed 6 pages (overall, i.e., including references) in IEEE, 10pt font, 2-column format (same templates as main conference)

Publication/Proceedings

The papers accepted in the workshop will appear and indexed in the IEEE digital libraries (Xplore).
